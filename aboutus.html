ABOUT US

Who are we?

Adorn Jewelery Shop -previously known as Adorn Jewels- is a private owned jewellery store. It started out as an online store before a physical store was attained.
We pride ourselves in being the one stop jewelery shop that meets all your needs in terms of jewelery while being relatively affordable and effcient sell quality jewellery.

What do we Offer

As your one stop jewellery shop, we sell a variety of products which include but aren't restricted to:
  • Non-tarnish chains and pendants
  • Custom jewellery
  • Beaded jewellery(on request)
  • Designer jewellery

Goals

Our goal is to become an internationally known brand that specializes in our own custom made jewellery.
